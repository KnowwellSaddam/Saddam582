Basics of Space Management for a Home Office

Business , Personal Development , Professional Development

Oluwaseun Adebayo-Omoju 45 min
  • Overview
  • Questions
  • Reviews
With so many of us working from home due to COVID-19, Oluwaseun Adebayo-Omoju wants to share her insight on the basics of Space Management. We all have limited space available. Oluwaseun will get you thinking about your personal and work spaces, what's in them, and how you use them. Her tips can help you take control of your personal and work spaces to make them - and you - more productive!

This is even more helpful for those in small urban apartments. If Oluwaseun was able to create productive work/study space for herself in a dormitory room shared with 6 other people, she can help you figure out your unique situation!!

    • Space Management in regard to personal and work spaces in your home
    • Value of applying space management principles to your home office
    • Study your space and how you interact with it
    • Space optimization provides increased productivity within the space
    • Quick Tips to Optimize your spaces: DCAP Principle
    • Make your space work for you
